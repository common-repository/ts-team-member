== Description ==

**TS Team Member** is a powerful yet easy-to-use WordPress plugin designed to help you create stunning and customizable team member showcases. Whether you need to highlight your team, staff, or employees, this plugin allows you to do so in a stylish and professional manner. 

One of the key features of TS Team Member is its **live editing** functionality, which enables you to visually design your showcase directly from the WordPress customizer or editor. With the live preview feature, you can see real-time changes as you adjust layouts, colors, and display options. This ensures that the final output is perfectly tailored to match your website's design and aesthetic.

TS Team Member also provides **shortcode support**, enabling you to easily embed team showcases anywhere on your website, such as posts, pages, or widget areas. Each team member profile can include a photo, bio, contact information, and social media links, making it easier for visitors to connect with your team.

Additionally, the plugin is highly customizable, offering a variety of layout options, grid or list views, and styling controls to ensure that your team section integrates seamlessly with your site design. Whether you're managing a small business or a large corporate team, **TS Team Member** is the perfect solution to showcase your team with style and efficiency.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `ts-team-member` plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to the 'TS Team' section in your WordPress dashboard to begin creating your team member profiles.
4. Use the live editor to customize your team member showcase.
5. Place the `[tsteam_showcase id={your-id}]` shortcode on any page or post to display your team.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I add a team showcase to my page? =

Simply use the `[tsteam_showcase id={your-id}]` shortcode on any page, post, or widget area where you'd like the team showcase to appear.

= Can I customize the layout of the team member profiles? =

Yes, the plugin offers multiple layout options and styling controls, allowing you to display your team in a grid or list view and customize the look to match your theme.

= Does the plugin support live editing? =

Yes, the plugin includes a live editing feature, allowing you to see real-time changes as you customize your team showcase.
